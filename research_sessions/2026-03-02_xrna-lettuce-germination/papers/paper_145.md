## Key Findings

### Lettuce Germination Gene Expression Time Course
- Standard experimental design: seeds sampled at 6, 12, 18, and 24 hours after imbibition
- Tissue-specific sampling: endosperm cap, cotyledon tip endosperm, cotyledon, and radicle
- Radicle emergence typically occurs 18-24h after imbibition at permissive temperatures

### Key Characterized Lettuce Germination Genes
1. **LsNCED4**: ABA biosynthesis enzyme; causal QTL gene for thermoinhibition (Htg6.1 locus)
   - Induced by high temperature imbibition in thermoinhibition-susceptible genotypes
   - Results in elevated seed ABA content
   - This is the main ABA biosynthesis control point in lettuce germination

2. **LsGA3ox1**: GA 3-beta-hydroxylase; control point for light-regulated GA biosynthesis
   - Light (red light via phytochrome) induces LsGA3ox1 expression
   - Produces bioactive GA for endosperm weakening and radicle elongation

3. **LsRbohC1**: NADPH oxidase producing superoxide radicals
   - Overexpression increases ROS in radicle and endosperm cap, promotes germination
   - Knockdown reduces ROS and delays germination
   - ROS involved in cell wall loosening (endosperm weakening) and signaling

4. **Endo-beta-mannanase gene family**: Multiple members in lettuce genome
   - Mobilizes endosperm cell walls during/after germination
   - Expression increases in micropylar and lateral endosperm following radicle emergence
   - Key enzyme for endosperm cap weakening

5. **LsXTH family**: Xyloglucan endotransglucosylases
   - 10 LsXTH genes expressed in endosperm cap during germination
   - LsXTH43 highest expression during germination; upregulated 2-fold by high temperature

### PIF3 in Endosperm
- PIF3 in endosperm represses CYP707A1 (ABA catabolic gene)
- Promotes endosperm ABA accumulation and release into embryo
- Temperature signal integration at PIF3 level

## Critical Implications for xRNA Project
- Lettuce germination involves coordinated ABA/GA/ROS signaling in specific tissues
- The 18-24h window for radicle emergence is the critical period for xRNA action
- LsNCED4 (ABA) and LsGA3ox1 (GA) are the master regulators in lettuce
- xRNA-mediated PP2C downregulation would affect the pathway DOWNSTREAM of ABA perception
- If PP2C47 is Clade B (not A), it would not interact with this ABA-GA axis at all
- The ROS pathway (LsRbohC1) represents a parallel germination control mechanism not targeted by xRNA
- Cell wall weakening enzymes (mannanase, XTH) are effectors of the hormonal signal — not direct xRNA targets

## Limitations
- These are individual gene studies, not genome-wide expression profiles
- No single lettuce study profiles ALL xRNA target genes simultaneously
- Time-course data mostly at tissue level, not single-cell resolution
