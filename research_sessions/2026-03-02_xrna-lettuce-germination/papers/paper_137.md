## Key Findings

### miRNAs Dynamically Regulated During Imbibition
- 12 miRNA families downregulated during imbibition: miR156, miR159, miR164, miR166, miR167, miR168, miR169, miR172, miR319, miR393, miR394, miR397
- 4 miRNA families upregulated: miR398, miR408, miR528, miR529
- miR159 targets MYB33/MYB101 (GA signaling during germination)
- miR160 targets ARF10/ARF16 (auxin response during germination)
- miR156 and miR172 are part of the age pathway (vegetative-reproductive transition)

### DCL1-ABA Connection
- Two ABA supersensitive germination mutants (absg1, absg2) are alleles of DCL1 and HEN1
- DCL1/HEN1 mutants show upregulation of ABA-responsive genes
- This directly connects small RNA biogenesis machinery to ABA-mediated germination control
- **Implication for xRNA**: Exogenous tRFs entering through DCL1-independent pathway might bypass this regulation

### ta-siRNA Pathway in Germination
- miR390-TAS3-ARF pathway active during germination
- ta-siRNAs generated through miRNA-guided cleavage of TAS transcripts
- SGS3 stabilizes precursors; RDR6 generates dsRNA; DCL4 produces 21-nt ta-siRNAs
- 58 miRNAs from 30 families identified at different germination stages
- 15 miRNAs and targets differentially expressed between dry seeds and 12h/24h/48h imbibition

### Hormone-Small RNA Crosstalk in Germination
- ABA, GA, and auxin signaling all have small RNA regulatory components
- miR159 promotes GA signaling by targeting negative regulators
- miR167 modulates auxin signaling through ARF6/ARF8
- miR393 targets TIR1 (auxin receptor) — downregulated during germination (releasing auxin signaling)

## Critical Implications for xRNA Project
- Seeds have an active and dynamic small RNA regulatory system during germination
- The DCL1-HEN1 mutant phenotype (ABA supersensitivity) shows that disrupting small RNA processing can dramatically alter germination via ABA
- xRNA tRFs would enter a system already engaged in active small RNA-mediated gene regulation
- The miR159-MYB33 (GA) and miR393-TIR1 (auxin) pathways could interact with xRNA-mediated silencing
- Small RNA levels change dramatically during the first 48h of imbibition — timing of xRNA application matters
