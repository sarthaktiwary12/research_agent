## Abstract
Removal of H3K9me2 from gene bodies by the jmjC histone demethylase IBM1/JMJ25 inhibits DNA methylation and derepresses gene expression. This study analyzed the function of a closely related homolog of IBM1/JMJ25, namely JMJ24. JMJ24 antagonizes IBM1/JMJ25 function and interacts with RNAi pathways for gene silencing. The balance between JMJ24 and JMJ25 activities controls the chromatin state at specific gene loci, regulating the interplay between histone demethylation, DNA methylation, and gene silencing pathways.
