## Key Findings

### Experimental Design
- Lettuce cv. 'Jianyexianfeng No. 1'
- Compared: dry seeds, germinated seeds at 15C, seeds at 15C after 48h imbibition at 25C, germinated at 25C in KNO3, and ungerminated at 25C (thermoinhibited)
- Individual seeds sampled at point of germination (not bulk)
- Storage proteins (>50% total) removed by PEG precipitation to detect lower-abundance proteins

### Proteomic Results
- **108 protein spots** changed >2-fold (P < 0.05) in at least one germination treatment
- 19 proteins increased and 1 decreased specifically during germination
- These germination-associated proteins had higher abundance in germinated conditions vs. thermoinhibited seeds

### Key Pathways Identified
- **Mevalonate (MVA) pathway for isoprenoid biosynthesis**: Directly involved in germination and thermoinhibition regulation
  - MVA pathway produces precursors for GA biosynthesis (germination-promoting)
  - Also produces precursors for sesquiterpenes and sterols
  - **Connects to beta-caryophyllene synthase target**: MVA pathway intermediates feed into both GA and sesquiterpene production
- **Methionine metabolism and ethylene production**: Ethylene promotes germination; methionine is precursor
- **Lipid mobilization**: Energy provision during early germination
- **Cell elongation**: Required for radicle protrusion
- **Aldehyde detoxification**: Oxidative stress management

### Cell Defense and Rescue Proteins
- Proteins involved in cell defense and rescue were the MOST NUMEROUS category increasing during imbibition at both temperatures
- This supports the defense-growth dynamic during germination

### qRT-PCR Validation
- Gene expression of candidate proteins confirmed by qRT-PCR
- Combined proteomic and transcriptomic data

## Critical Implications for xRNA Project
- **MVA pathway involvement directly connects to multiple xRNA targets**:
  - HMGR: rate-limiting MVA pathway enzyme
  - Beta-caryophyllene synthase: uses MVA-derived FPP as substrate
  - GA biosynthesis: MVA pathway provides GGPP precursor
- MVA pathway is ACTIVE during lettuce germination and involved in thermoinhibition
- Defense proteins being the most abundant category during imbibition supports the defense reallocation model
- Individual seed sampling methodology is important: avoids averaging across heterogeneous populations
- **This is the most directly relevant molecular study for the xRNA-lettuce project**

## Limitations
- Proteomic study (proteins, not transcripts) — different coverage than RNA-seq
- Chinese lettuce cultivar, not widely used Western cultivars
- 108 differentially abundant proteins is a relatively small number (limited 2D gel resolution)
- Cannot search for specific xRNA target proteins without full protein list
